Our Story

Founded in 2025 by UiTM students who were tired of limited study spaces on campus, StudySip was created to provide the perfect environment for focused study sessions.

We've designed every aspect of our cafe with students in mind - from the comfortable seating to the study-friendly lighting, from our affordable student pricing to our convenient evening hours that align with student schedules.

Our baristas are trained to make your drinks exactly how you need them to fuel your study session, whether that's an extra shot of espresso or a calming matcha latte.
